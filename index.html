Do Yoga Err Day

All day 'err Day I do Yoga. If I'm at work, I'm doing Yoga. At the park? Yoga. Shop? Yoga. Yoga? Yoga.

Of course when I do I yoga I do yoga, you think, but what if some teachers didn't do Yoga when they did Yoga? Well, regardless, that's not me, because when I do Yoga I do Yoga, and when I don't do Yoga, I'm thinking about it and wondering how I might be able to do something like Yoga, and incorporate it into my life.
